# Printable CAD Files

## Repository Files

### 1. `Net_Pot.stl`
A reusable, printable net pot to secure your plants within the hydroponic tower.  
- Designed for **2" grow sponges or growing medium**.  
- Print as many as needed for the number of slots in your tower.

---

### 2. `Tower_Lid.stl`
The **lid** for the hydroponic tower.

---

### 3. `Tower_Rain_Head_Plate.stl`
A **plate** designed to evenly distribute water flow throughout the hydroponic tower.

---

### 4. `Tower_Body.stl`
The **main body** of the hydroponic tower.  
- Scaled to fit up to **5 plants** at a time.
