Introduction

The biggest challenges of raising plants in a dorm can be giving them the appropriate habitat to thrive in and remembering to water/care for them appropriately. The Smart Greenhouse could resolve these challenges for small to midsize plants by adjusting the temperature, humidity, and exposure to sunlight inside of the enclosure, as well as allowing the user to set up all kinds of care reminders. The RPi would have to be attached to the appropriate sensors (temperature, humidity, and brightness), and the user could configure the greenhouse to maintain the desired environment using a settings panel displayed on the RPi when the plant is first placed in the greenhouse, and adjust the settings as necessary at any time. The RPi could give alerts to the user on the screen to water the plants, change soil, add fertilizer, or any other kinds of care instructions according to the user's settings.

Project Objective:

  • Ability to remotely monitor plant.
  • Ability to manually control light and humidity levels inside greenhouse.
  • Remotely control hydroponic tower water pump.
